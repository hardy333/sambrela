Round Colors

Complete colorful rounds and enjoy a colorful journey!

The main goal is to collect all round balls that are your color. In every round you are given a task to collect a certain amount of balls that are exactly your color. If you touch any other colored balls you will lose points. You should complete the task within time. But you can collect bonus time as well.

Catch your friendly color ball, don't touch your enemies, perfect timing is the best winning tactic for you! Interesting stages and best quality levels in the scene.

How to play:

  • Collect all round balls that are your color. Don’t touch any other colored balls, otherwise you will lose points.
